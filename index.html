ImageBaler
by Ramblin Jackson

Web-ready image compression done fast — no software required.

Batch compress hundreds of images in seconds — no uploads, no installs, nothing leaves your device.

1
Drop Your Images
Drag a folder into the box to import all images including subfolders. Use browse files for individual images, or browse subfolder to pick a single folder.
2
Choose Your Settings
Select output format, max width, and quality. Not sure? The defaults are optimized for web use — just leave them as-is.
3
Compress All
Hit Compress All and wait for all images to finish. You'll see the file size savings update in real time.
4
Download Your ZIP
Name your ZIP file, then hit Download ZIP. Your compressed images and a log file will be bundled together.
